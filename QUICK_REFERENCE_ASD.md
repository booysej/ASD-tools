## рҹ“Ҡ Task Clarity Rules

вң… **Good Task**: "Review section 3.2 of report, fix grammar errors"
- Specific location
- Clear action
- Measurable outcome

вқҢ **Vague Task**: "Work on report"
- Where?
- What action?
- When complete?

## рҹ”„ If Routine Disrupted
