# Email Template: Asking for Clarification

**Subject:** Clarification needed on [TOPIC]

**To:** [NAME]

**Body:**

Hi [NAME],

I want to make sure I understand [TOPIC] correctly before proceeding.

Could you please clarify the following points:

1. [SPECIFIC QUESTION 1]
   - What I understood: [YOUR INTERPRETATION]
   - What I'm unsure about: [SPECIFIC CONFUSION]

2. [SPECIFIC QUESTION 2]
   - Current assumption: [WHAT YOU THINK]
   - Need confirmation on: [WHAT TO CONFIRM]

3. [SPECIFIC QUESTION 3 - if needed]

**Expected outcome:** Once I understand these points, I will [SPECIFIC ACTION].

**Timeline:** I plan to work on this [WHEN], so a response by [DEADLINE] would be helpful.

Thank you for your patience with my questions. Clear communication helps me deliver better results.

Best regards,
[YOUR NAME]

---

## Usage Notes:
- Replace all [BRACKETED] sections with specific information
- Remove any unused question sections
- Keep language direct and professional
- Avoid apologizing excessively - asking for clarity is professional
