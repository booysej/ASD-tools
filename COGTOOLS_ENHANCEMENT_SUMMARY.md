# 🧠 CogTools Enhancement Summary

This document summarizes the comprehensive enhancements made to transform CogTools from a basic prototype into a fully functional, useful cognitive assistance suite designed specifically for individuals with ASD (Autism Spectrum Disorder) and their families.
